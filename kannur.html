KANNUR

Tourists come from all parts of the country to embark on the range of exotic natural wonders and gorgeous hilly destinations. Kannur is home to a plethora of places to visit starting from being a spiritual getaway hub, adventure-filled trekking trips, thrilling experiences for kids, and idyllic relaxed nature tours. The age-old forts and museums will be perfect destinations for architecture buffs while the shopping markets at the fort road will quench the thirst of all shopaholics. Moreover, the scenic parks will tranquillize your senses, and the secluded beaches located far from the chaotic city life will give you a sense of relaxation. Head out on this serene and sublime tour of Kannur with your loved ones to gather some priced memoirs that will be cherished all through your life. Check out this list of some well-curated places to visit in Kannur and plan for some amazing days in God's own country, Kerala.
