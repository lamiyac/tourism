kottayam

ottayam is blessed with nature’s bounty in abundance. It is the pride of God’s own country with its beautiful churches, charming nearby hill stations, significant temples, and refreshing waterfalls. The Western Ghats and the backwaters add to the beauty to this undisturbed, rustic town of Kerala. The numerous plantations – rubber, coconut, palm, spices and the flora make for lush green landscapes that are totally stunning. All these factors, aside from it being strikingly beautiful, make it as one of the must visit destinations in Kerala. Though a handful, the tourist places in Kottayam guarantee a peaceful retreat. It is not one of most famous towns of Kerala, but is no less beautiful than the likes of Munnar, Alleppey or Kanyakumari. If you gotta see it to believe it, here’s a round up of quaint and charming places to visit in and around Kottayam.

1.Vaikom – Home To Many Secluded Haunts

2.Elaveezha Poonchira – Nature At Its Best

17. Neendoor Subrahmanya Swami Temple – Pay A Visit

18.Ettumanoor Mahadeva Temple – Admire The Beauty

19. Tomb Of Saint Alphonsa – Most Revered Place To Visit

20.Clay Art Cafe – Relish Tasty Cakes With Artwork

22.. Malliyoor Sri Maha Ganapathi Temple – A Grand One

23.Thangal Para – A Beautiful Hill
